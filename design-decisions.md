## Why Private Subnets?
To prevent direct internet access and reduce attack surface.

## Why NAT Gateway?
Allows outbound internet access without exposing EC2 instances.

## Why Application Load Balancer?
Provides health checks, traffic distribution, and high availability.

## Why Auto Scaling Group?
Ensures scalability and automatic recovery.

## Why Bastion Host?
Provides secure and controlled access to private EC2 instances.

## Console-Based Approach
Manual AWS Console configuration was intentionally used to validate
networking and security behavior before automation.
